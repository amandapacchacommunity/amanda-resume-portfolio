About Amanda Paccha

I work at the intersection of risk management, compliance, governance, and data analytics. My career spans higher education, public administration, nonprofit leadership, and cultural institutions — where I have focused on strengthening institutional accountability, improving compliance systems, and translating complex data into strategic insight.

Professional Focus

Risk & Compliance

I design and support enterprise risk management programs, compliance frameworks, and policy systems that help institutions identify emerging risks and strengthen accountability.

Investigations & Ethics

My work includes supporting misconduct investigations, compliance reporting, and case documentation aligned with organizational policies and regulatory standards.

Data & Reporting

I build dashboards, analyze institutional datasets, and translate operational information into executive-level reporting that supports strategic decision-making.

Professional Background

My career has included roles in higher education, major cultural institutions, nonprofit organizations, and city government. Across these environments I have supported governance initiatives including:

  • Enterprise risk register development and mitigation strategy
  • Clery Act compliance and federal reporting
  • Employee relations and workplace investigations support
  • Compensation analytics and pay equity analysis
  • Compliance monitoring and internal audit preparation
  • Policy development and training programs

These roles have allowed me to work closely with leadership teams, legal counsel, human resources, student affairs, finance, and external partners to ensure institutions maintain ethical operations and strong compliance cultures.

Approach to Work
